# HR Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing employee attrition and workforce trends to help organizations understand the key factors influencing employee turnover. The dashboard provides actionable insights that support HR teams in improving employee retention and workforce planning.

## Business Problem

Employee attrition can significantly impact organizational productivity, hiring costs, and overall business performance. The objective of this project is to identify patterns behind employee turnover and uncover factors contributing to attrition.

## Dataset Information

* Total Employees: 1,470
* Multiple employee-related attributes including:

  * Age
  * Gender
  * Education
  * Department
  * Job Role
  * Monthly Income
  * Years at Company
  * Attrition Status

## Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* Microsoft Excel
* DAX
* Data Cleaning & Transformation

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Employees
* Active Employees
* Attrition Count
* Attrition Rate
* Average Age
* Average Salary
* Average Years at Company

## Analysis Performed

* Department-wise Attrition Analysis
* Job Role-wise Attrition Analysis
* Gender Distribution Analysis
* Age Group Analysis
* Salary Slab Analysis
* Education-wise Attrition Trends
* Employee Tenure Analysis

## Key Insights

* Identified departments with the highest employee turnover.
* Analyzed the relationship between salary and attrition.
* Discovered employee age groups with higher resignation rates.
* Evaluated the impact of work experience on employee retention.

## Business Impact

The insights generated from this dashboard can help HR teams:

* Improve employee retention strategies
* Reduce recruitment and training costs
* Identify high-risk employee segments
* Make data-driven workforce decisions

## Project Outcome

This dashboard transforms raw HR data into meaningful business insights, enabling organizations to better understand workforce behavior and improve employee retention.
